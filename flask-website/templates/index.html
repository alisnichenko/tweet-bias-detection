What is this about?

This is about machine learning and misinformation. We see Twitter

as a very powerful platform, however, in this period of constant

misinformation and polarization, it is difficult to extract

something useful from everyday interactions with social media.

We wanted to change that. And that is why we developed this project.

What did we do?

We designed a prototype of an idea that has all the reasons to succeed

We did the research necessary (described below), and started coding.

We developed a simple convolutional neural network that attempts

to extract features from existing Tweets from accounts, that were

known the be biased. We consulted the adfontesmedia.com

websited to obtain accurate information about biases to train our model.

We then were able to finish our machine learning model to classify

random tweets and phrases. Finally, we presented a working prototype

that can be tested here.
